Privacy Policy

From: Colten Sus

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and manage your personal data when you use our application. By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Our application integrates with Pinterest to provide you with access to various features. To achieve this, we collect the following data:

  • Profile/Account Information: To authenticate your identity and allow you to sign in.
  • Control Panel Access: To manage and access your Pinterest account through our app.
  • Pin Creation & Scheduling: To enable creating and scheduling Pins.
  • Publishing Content: To publish content directly to Pinterest.
  • Reporting: To retrieve data about your account for analytics and insights.
  • Ad Campaign Management: To create and manage ad campaigns for your account or users.
  • Pinner App Features: To enhance your experience as a Pinterest user (Pinner).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide secure access to your Pinterest account.
  • Allow you to manage your Pins, campaigns, and reports.
  • Enhance your user experience by offering integrated features for content creation, scheduling, and analytics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or to ensure the functionality of our application.

4. Data Security

We implement strict measures to protect your data and ensure its security during transmission and storage.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time. For assistance, please contact our support team.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at support@coltensus.com.
